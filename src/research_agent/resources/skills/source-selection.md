# Source Selection

For market and company research:
- Prefer official company pages, filings, investor material, product documentation, and reputable reporting.
- Distinguish verified facts from inference.
- Avoid treating repeated coverage of the same press release as independent confirmation.
- If sources conflict or evidence is thin, say so explicitly.
